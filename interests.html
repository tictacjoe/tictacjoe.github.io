Cyber AI

My primary professional interest over the past few years has been developing ways to apply techniques emerging from the world of AI to the practice of Cyber Security. My last few roles have been about developing and leading teams of Security Architects, Security Engineers and Developers, and Data Scientists towards this end on behalf of a global telecommunications company. As a start-up division within a large corporation, my team and I wore many hats as we brought our products to market, requiring us to be hands-on in development, engineering, operations, sales, and management in support of the cyber security platform we built and delivered. In our first couple of years of operation we brought in over $50 million in contracts to the business and launched the platform in the U.S., Europe, and Asia. We also leveraged the serverless architecture technology of AWS as we refined and developed the system, thereby drastically reducing costs. The platform was well received by industry analysts, greatly improved our company's position as a cyber security providor, and currently serves as both a product for sale to industry and an internal tool used by the company for its internal cyber security practice.
