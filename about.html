Knowing Things

Its fascinating to me that we seem to be on the verge of a new way of knowing and understanding our world. You can see the dissonance created by these changes all around us. Well-read, intelligent people can completely disagree on the most fundamental points. Ideas that were matter-of-fact just a decade ago have suddenly become controversial. Ideas and behaviors that would have been indefensible or inexplicable in the past are now supported by entire communities. And yet, we are more aware of each other than ever.
